# Roshidere
## "THE ICE PRINCESS AND THE CANDY BOY"

### Season 1 — Game Plan

A 14-chapter, 4-act story following **Jesús "Chuy" Hernández Torres** as he arrives at Seirei Private Academy carrying nothing but Pulparindos, a Virgen de Guadalupe chain, and a smile that doesn't know it's about to be tested.

| Act | Title | Chapters | Theme |
|-----|-------|----------|-------|
| **I** | Arrival | 1–3 | *"Nosotros los Nobles"* — A new world, a new school, first cracks |
| **II** | Friendship | 4–7 | *"Échale Ganas, Mijo"* — Bonds form, home unravels |
| **III** | Ambition | 8–11 | *"Candy Boy"* — Reputation rises, Yuki strikes |
| **IV** | Breaking Point | 12–14 | *"¿Por Qué No Moriste Tú?"* — The smile cracks |
