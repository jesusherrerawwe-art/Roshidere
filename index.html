Season 1

Jesús "Chuy" Hernández Torres lands in Tokyo with the echo of his father's voice still warm in his ears. At Seirei Private Academy, he becomes the "Candy Boy" — a tourist attraction wrapped in warmth that the institution doesn't know how to process. But behind the smile, a mother is drowning in grief, a princess is watching from behind walls of ice, and a girl with plans for her brother is sharpening her knives.

Fourteen chapters. Four acts. One golden chain that never comes off.
